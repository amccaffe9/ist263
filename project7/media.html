Handsome Dan

Handsome Dan is the University of Yale's mascot and currently has about 40,000 followers on Instagram. He gets to walk around campus and is trained to go to games and support his athletic teams. He is an American Bulldog and is roughly two years old, and took the reins of Handsome Dan during COVID in 2020. Handsome Dan is most popular among the sports teams when he goes to the games and gets a lot of pets from every team for supporting them.

Butler Blue IV

Like Handsome Dan, Butler Blue is a mascot but for Butler University instead. Blue has 54 thousand followers on Instagram and got a lot of notoriety after the changing of the collar ceremony at a basketball game taking over his Uncle Trip's role as the mascot.

Bowl Dog

Theo loves his bowls. Sometimes, dogs develop specific obsessions with certain toys and this dog happened to develop them for bowls. It brings out the most energy in him. When the owners open the dishwasher, he will do running to get the clean bowls and play with them. Maybe there's a reason they're called bowl-dogs (haha).
