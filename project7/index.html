Welcome!!!

Welcome to the Bulldog Archive, a website dedicated to providing information about one of the most adorable, laziest, craziest breeds of dogs: The Bulldog. Oftentimes confused with pugs, the Bulldog is famous for its wrinkles and chubby body. This website aims to inform about the bulldog, the issues the breed faces, and some awesome bulldogs who have taken the hearts of social media.

History

Bulldogs have a long history and even date back to 5th century England, and they were bred with aggressive traits to help with livestock. Eventually the aggressiveness of the bulldogs led to entertainment and the “bull” name of the dogs, as they were baited against a bull and would attack the bull's nose until it had pulled the bull ot the ground or the bull killed the dog.
